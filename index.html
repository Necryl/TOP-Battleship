This game is called BattleShip!

There are two boards, one for you and one for the enemy. You can place your ships on your board. The enemy will do the same.

To place a ship you need to select it. Click one of the grey blocks on your side of the board to select a ship. When a ship is selected it gets a blue outline. If you are on mobile, you can click on the board and drag to position your ship. If you are on a desktop/laptop, you can right-click on the board to change the orientation of your ship.

At the beginning of the game neither of you can see the other's ships or their positions.

Now you can click on the "Start" button to start the game. Each player takes turn to fire at a cell in the enemy board. To do this, simply click on a cell in the enemy board. If you get a hit, that cell will turn black. Else it will turn blue.

Once you hit a ship, don't forget to fire at the cells around it to hit the rest of the ship. The ships are only one cell wide but they can be upto four cells long!

Once all the cells of the ship are hit, the ship turns red to indicate that it has sunk.

Also there are status indicators for both players that show which ships are remaining and which have sunk.

Once the enemy loses all of their ships, you win the game!

Let's get started!

Extra Information:

The "Randomize" button under your board will place ships randomly across your board, although it is not very strategic.

The "Reset" button will clear your board so you place the ships in a new way. Keep in mind, you can always select a ship and click on an empty cell on the board to reposition it.
